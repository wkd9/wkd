About the Group

Wael Al-Daqal is a co-founder, brand owner and franchisor with a bachelor's degree in Business Studies from Canterbury Chris Church University (UK), followed by a master's in International Commercial Arbitration from British institutions in Egypt.

In addition to his current roles, Wael Al Daqal also serves as a Franchise Systems Advisor at the Small and Medium Enterprises Authority.

Wael currently holds three franchise licenses, two of which are master franchises: one from Florina, a company with a 50-year history and over 140 branches across Saudi Arabia, and the other from mmmm Burger. The third is exclusive to Madinah from Richy, a Saudi menswear company with a long-standing history of over 60 years.

Executive roles and memberships:

  • Executive Member of the Saudi-Korean Business Council
  • Vice Chairman of the Franchise Committee at the Federation of Saudi Chambers
  • Chairman of the Entrepreneurship Committee at the Chamber of Commerce
  • Member of the National Entrepreneurship Committee at the Federation of Saudi Chambers

Brands Owned:
