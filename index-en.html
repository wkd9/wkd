Who We Are

WKD Group is powered by Saudi expertise, prominently led by its CEO Wael Al Daqal, a co-founder, brand owner, and franchisor with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Studies from Canterbury Christ Church University (UK), followed by a Master’s in International Commercial Arbitration from British institutions in Egypt.

In addition to his current roles, Wael Al Daqal also serves as a Franchise Systems Advisor at the Small and Medium Enterprises Authority.

Wael currently holds three franchise licenses, two of which are master franchises: one from Florina, a company with a 50-year history and over 140 branches across Saudi Arabia, and the other from MMMM Burger. The third is exclusive to Madinah City from Richy, a Saudi menswear company with over 60 years in business.

Executive roles and memberships:

  • Executive Member of the Saudi-Korean Business Council
  • Vice Chairman of the Franchise Committee at the Federation of Saudi Chambers
  • Chairman of the Entrepreneurship Committee at the Chamber of Commerce
  • Member of the National Entrepreneurship Committee at the Federation of Saudi Chambers

Brands Owned:

  • Florina Company
  • Willies Restaurant
  • The Mountain Stable (horse business)
  • Drive-Thru Real Estate Project

Shareholder in international companies specializing in expanding Saudi businesses abroad:

  • British Company – Takeoff: Provides integrated consulting in business development, project management, and HR to support growth and operational efficiency in the British market, and helps Saudi brands enter that market.
  • Chinese Company – The Solution Bridge: A Chinese firm with 14+ years of experience supporting brands between Saudi Arabia and China for professional market entry.
  • South African Company – SetupAZ: Assists companies in establishing operations in South Africa with full legal, visa, tax, and compliance services, also aiding Saudi companies to enter that market.
  • Korean Company – Korea In: Supports Saudi brands to successfully expand into the South Korean market with comprehensive services and expert local guidance.
