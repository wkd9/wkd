About the Group

WKD Group, owned and led by Mr. Wael Khalid Abdul Qader Daqal for 10 years,
holds the franchise rights for Florina, MMMM, and Ritchi in Saudi Arabia.
Mr. Daqal serves as Vice-President of the Franchise Committee at the Saudi Chambers Council,
is an Executive Member of the Saudi-Korean Council, Chair of the Entrepreneurship Committee in
Madinah Chamber, and an investor partner in Take Off Company.
He also contributed to establishing the Saudi Franchise Law under the Small & Medium Enterprises Authority.
We aspire to earn your admiration and to build lasting business relationships with you.
